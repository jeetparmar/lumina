The Story

I Don't Take Photos,
I Craft Memories

Every frame is a silent poem — a conversation between light, shadow, and the human soul.

Born in Prague, trained in Florence, and now based between New York and the world, Alex Mercer has spent over a decade chasing light across five continents. His work has been featured in National Geographic, Vogue, and The New Yorker.

What drives him isn't technology or technique — it's the obsessive pursuit of the single perfect moment when everything aligns: the light, the subject, the story.
